## Harness the Power of AI for Flashcard Creation

Welcome to the future of flashcard creation with our **AI Generate** feature. Smart Study takes the heavy lifting off your shoulders, 
allowing you to generate flashcards in seconds—automatically!

## Meet Chronicle: Your AI Flashcard Assistant

On the **AI Generate** page, you’ll be introduced to Chronicle, the AI-powered assistant built into Smart Study. Simply enter your topic 
of interest into the text field, and let the AI do the rest.

It’s as easy as typing a phrase like, “Generate flashcards about Binary Search Trees.” In just 10-20 seconds, your custom flashcards will 
appear, ready for you to study. No need to manually enter information—Chronicle generates high-quality flashcards in a snap.
