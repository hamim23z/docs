<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What is Smart Study?" defaultOpen={true}>
    Smart Study is a website where students can create flashcards from scratch or use our AI, ChronicleAI, to generate flashcards. Students will also have access to study materials such as YouTube videos, worksheets, and pre-made cards.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How can I create flashcards?" defaultOpen={true}>
    You can create flashcards manually by typing in questions and answers, or use ChronicleAI to generate flashcards based on the content you provide.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I use pre-made flashcards?" defaultOpen={true}>
    Yes, we offer a variety of pre-made flashcards that you can use. These are available for different subjects and topics, especially for computer science and engineering.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Is Smart Study free?" defaultOpen={true}>
    Smart Study is currently free for all users. In the future, we plan to offer different membership plans with more features.
  </Accordion>
