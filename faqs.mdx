### 
If your question is not listed here, please feel free to contact us and we will reach out to you as soon as possible.

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What is Smart Study?" defaultOpen={true}>
    Smart Study is a website where students can create flashcards from scratch or use our AI, ChronicleAI, to generate flashcards. Students will also have access to study materials such as YouTube videos, worksheets, and pre-made cards.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How can I create flashcards?" defaultOpen={true}>
    Creating flashcards has never been easier. Upon logging in, you will be automatically redirected to the self generate flashcard page. On this page, you can make as many flashcards as you want. There is also a button that redirects you to the AI where you can enter a topic and flashcards will be made for you. All of these flashcards can be saved to your own collection.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I use pre-made flashcards?" defaultOpen={true}>
    Yes! Pre-made flashcards will be available. However, the team is still working on this feature due to the number of topics. Rest assured, this feature will be available to everyone very soon.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Is Smart Study free to use?" defaultOpen={true}>
    Yes, Smart Study is currently free to use. However, we do plan to introduce paid features later down the road as we gain more users and more traction but nothing anytime soon.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Do you offer customer support? " defaultOpen={true}>
    Yes, there are many different ways to contact us. The first being through the Contact Form linked here. It can also be found on the top of every page. The second way to contact us is by emailing us at smartstudycs0@gmail.com. We will respond to your inquiry as soon as possible.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
